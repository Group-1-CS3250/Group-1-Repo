<h2>Culture</h2>
<ol>
<li>Trust</li>
   <pr>No fear of judgment from other team members when your opinion is voiced.<br>
   Have trust in each person's capabilities and commitment.</pr>
<li>Conflict</li>
   Open Discussion among the group.
   All conflicts should be resolved with the entire group.
   Each team member will have an equal say in final decisions.
<li>Commitement</li>
<li>Accountability</li> 
   <pr>Have Stand-up Meetings<br>
      -Meeting where each person can share their progress, feelings, and any challenges they are facing, where each person must share.<br>
   Check-ins are consistently direct and effective. <br>
      -We will practice consistent check-ins for each group member that will catch any stress early to resolve conflicts. <br>
   Updates from each member <br>
      -If a member is unavailable, they must update others to ensure that plans can be changed before any conflict arises. <br>
   Review/notes for anyone who wasn't available <br>
      -Present team members will track meeting minutes that can be used to update any team member who is currently not present. </pr>
</ol>
<h2> Meetings </h2>
   <li> Times to meet</li>
   <pr> In-class meeting times will take priority over all else. <br>
   However, if stated or deemed necessary by the majority of the group, a consensus will be reached to meet on our communication platform.</pr>
<h2> Communication</h2>
<ol>
   <li> Methods of Communicaiton</li>
   <pr> Every group member will use Text Messaging as the main source of communication.<br>
   Microsoft Teams will be a backup form of communication if necessary.</pr>
   <li> Expected Time for Response</li>
   <pr> Each member will be expected to respond within 24 hours of initial response.</pr>
</ol>
<h2> Decisions</h2>
<ol>
   <li> Resolving conflict</li>
   <pr> Each decision will be made democratically, with everyone's opinions and voices heard.</pr>
</ol>
<h2> Development</h2>
<ol>
   <li> How will work be coordinated/completed </li>
   <pr> Tickets that each member will assign to themselves for work individually or collaboratively.</pr>
   <li>Consequences</li>
   <pr> Each team member should have the accountability and trust to say when they are struggling or may not be able to finish in time.<br>
      If a member isn't able to effectively communicate why they couldn't complete a task on their own, they will be required to share why they didn't meet their goal in front of the group.<br>
      Team members will all also suffer if someone isn't able to complete their goals as needed.</pr>
</ol>
<h2> Work Flow</h2>
<ol>
   <li> Process</li>
   <pr> We will set up a GitHub KanBan board that will contain tasks that are created by team members, as mentioned earlier in development, using tickets.<br>
   One person will be chosen to be a representative for the stakeholder to communicate any issues or problems to create tickets, and ask for clarifying. </pr>
</ol>
<h2>Agreemment Review</h2>
<pr>Agreement will be reviewed before each major project to ensure relevance of the agreement.</pr>
